Welcome to Our General Store and Bate Shop!

Location

Howdy! Welcome to Howard's Lakeside General Store! We're a one stop shop for fishing gear, snacks, and information about Lakeside. Come on in and chat a bit! We're right next to the docks on the east side of the lake.

We're open on weekends early so you can grab some bait, a snack, and some gear if you need it.

Even if you don't want to go fishing, we have you covered. Locals always bring a little extra for us to sell, so check out our Catch of the Day.
